Hedgehog Bread

Description

Freshly oven-baked german bread with melted cheese and tasty bacon!

Perfect for the small snack or a shared appetizer.


Ingredients

  • 1 large round bread loaf
  • (8 ounce) package shredded Swiss cheese
  • ½ cup chopped cooked bacon
  • ¼ cup butter
  • ¼ cup chopped garlic, or to taste
  • 1 (8 ounce) package Raclette cheese, sliced into pieces
  • 1tablespoon olive oil, or to taste
  • 1 pinch salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Cut a checkerboard pattern of 1-inch square segments into most of the loaf, leaving the bottom crust intact
  3. Combine Swiss cheese, bacon, butter, and garlic in a bowl
  4. Spoon the mixture between the loaf segments and stuff the loaf with slices of Raclette
  5. Drizzle olive oil all over the top; sprinkle with salt. Place loaf on a pizza stone
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until crust is golden brown, about 35 minutes
