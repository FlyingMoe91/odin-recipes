German Potato Salad

Description

Flavorful twist to a German classic, this potato salad gets a big boost of flavor from our ham.

Perfect for a light meal.


Ingredients

  • 3 pounds red potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 tablespoon neutral cooking oil
  • 1 (16 ounce) package Farmland® Sliced Ham, cut into strips about 1/2-inch wide
  • 1 cup diced white onion
  • ½ cup white vinegar
  • ½ cup Dijon mustard
  • ¼ cup white sugar
  • ¼ cup minced fresh chives
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Cook potatoes in boiling salted water until just tender, about 20 minutes
  2. While potatoes are cooking, heat pan over medium heat and add oil. When oil is hot, add sliced ham and cook until lightly browned with crispy, golden edges, about 5 minutes.
  3. Drain potatoes and allow to cool slightly. While still warm, gently mix potatoes in large bowl with all other ingredients. Taste for seasoning.
